# Look and Feel
* Style: "Vintage Library", the application should look like an old sheet of paper

## Color Scheme
* Main Background: #F2E8CF
* User Bubble: #829377
* Send Button: #BC6C4D
* Text Color: #3D3631
* Secondary Icons: #D1C7B0

## Fonts
* **For AI Responses (Body):** **[Crimson Pro](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Crimson+Pro)**
    * *Why:* It has a classic, "Oldstyle" feel that pairs perfectly with the Terracotta and Sage colors. It makes the AI feel like a wise mentor.
* **For Headers & Model Selector:** **[Space Grotesk](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Space+Grotesk)**
    * *Why:* It’s a "quirky" sans-serif. The slightly geometric shapes mirror the owl’s circular eyes and monocle, giving the UI a subtle "tech-meets-vintage" twist.

* Quick CSS/Style Guide:
* **AI Text:** `font-family: 'Lora', serif; font-weight: 400; color: #3D3631;`
* **User Text:** `font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif; font-weight: 500; color: #FFFFFF;`
* **Model Name:** `font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif; font-weight: 700; text-transform: uppercase;`
